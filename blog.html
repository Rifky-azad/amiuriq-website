Who We Are

Built by Builders, Driven by Purpose

Amiuriq Pvt Ltd is a Sri Lanka-based software development company delivering cutting-edge digital solutions to clients across the globe. Founded with a mission to make enterprise-grade technology accessible and impactful, we combine deep technical expertise with a human-centred approach to every project we undertake.

From our headquarters in Colombo, our distributed team of engineers, designers, data scientists, and strategists collaborate seamlessly — across time zones, across disciplines, and across borders — to deliver software that truly moves the needle for businesses.

Innovation First We invest in R&D so our clients always get solutions built on what works today and what will work tomorrow.
Client-Obsessed Every decision we make is filtered through one question: does this create real value for our clients?
Integrity in Every Line We build secure, maintainable, and honest software — no shortcuts, ever.

Our Environment

A Workspace That Energises

We believe where and how you work shapes what you build. Our environment is designed to spark creativity, remove friction, and fuel deep focus.

Modern Tech Stack

Every team member is equipped with premium hardware and licensed access to the best development tools, cloud platforms, and productivity software on the market — no excuses, just execution.

Open Collaboration Culture

Flat hierarchy means ideas flow freely. Whether you're a junior developer or a senior architect, your voice shapes product decisions. We hold weekly standups, design reviews, and open retrospectives.

Continuous Learning

We sponsor certifications, conference attendance, and internal knowledge-sharing sessions. Learning isn't a perk — it's built into how we operate, with dedicated time for growth every sprint.

Wellbeing-Centred

Mental health matters. We encourage healthy work rhythms, no-meeting focus blocks, and team socials. Burnout is not a badge of honour here — sustainable pace is how we build great things long-term.

Diverse & Inclusive

Our team spans multiple countries, backgrounds, and disciplines. Diversity isn't a checkbox — it directly improves the quality of our thinking, our products, and the solutions we deliver.

Recognition & Growth

Exceptional work is recognised publicly and rewarded meaningfully. Clear career ladders, performance reviews, and merit-based growth ensure that dedication always gets noticed.

Flexibility

Work on Your Terms

Productivity doesn't follow a 9-to-5. We trust our people to manage their time and deliver outstanding results, wherever they are.

Remote-First by Default

Our entire workflow — standups, code reviews, sprint planning, client demos — is built to work 100% remotely. No one is penalised for not being in the office.

Flexible Working Hours

Core overlap hours exist for collaboration, but beyond that you own your schedule. Early bird or night owl — as long as the work ships, we respect your rhythm.

Work From Anywhere

Whether you're in Colombo, a co-working space in Dubai, or your hometown — your location doesn't limit your role or your career growth at Amiuriq.

Unlimited Recharge Days

We offer flexible leave so team members can recharge without guilt. Rested minds write better code and design better products — it's that simple.

IT-Based Employee Support

Powered by Smart IT Systems

Our employees are supported by a robust internal IT infrastructure that automates the mundane, resolves issues fast, and keeps every team member fully equipped to do their best work.

Device & Equipment Management

All hardware is centrally managed through MDM (Mobile Device Management) systems. New hires receive pre-configured, fully set-up workstations on day one — zero setup friction, maximum productivity from the start.

24/7 Internal Helpdesk

Our IT support ticketing system ensures no issue goes unresolved. From software bugs to VPN access, our dedicated helpdesk team responds within 2 hours and resolves 90% of issues same-day.

Cloud Access & Licencing

Every developer gets immediate access to our cloud environments — AWS, GCP, Azure sandboxes — along with licensed access to JetBrains IDEs, Figma, Postman, and all essential SaaS tools, fully managed centrally.

Security & Access Control

Role-based access control (RBAC), SSO authentication, and mandatory MFA protect all company systems. Our security-first IT policy keeps team members and client data safe without adding workflow friction.

AI-Assisted Workflows

Internal AI tools automate repetitive tasks — from generating boilerplate code to summarising sprint notes and drafting client updates. Our team spends time on high-value work, not busywork.

Performance & Monitoring Dashboards

Transparent, real-time dashboards give every team member visibility into project health, deployment pipelines, and system uptime. Everyone stays informed — not just the managers.
