1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners about how visitors interact with their site.

Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your device. They are unique to your browser and typically contain anonymized identifiers that associate the cookie with a specific website and session.

Similar Technologies: In addition to cookies, we may use local storage, session storage, web beacons, and pixel tags. This policy covers all such technologies collectively referred to as "cookies" below.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly and securely
  • Remember your preferences and settings between visits
  • Understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Provide relevant content and a personalized experience
  • Prevent fraud and protect the security of our website and users

We respect your privacy and use cookies only as described in this policy. We will not use cookies to collect sensitive personal information without your explicit consent.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They are set in response to actions you take, such as submitting a form or setting your privacy preferences. You can set your browser to block these cookies, but parts of the website may not work as a result.

Analytics & Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to measure and improve the performance of our website. They help us understand which pages are popular, how visitors move around the site, and where they encounter errors. All data collected is aggregated and anonymized.

Preference Cookies

These cookies enable the website to remember choices you make (such as language preference, region, or accessibility settings) and provide enhanced, personalized features. They may be set by us or by third-party services we have added to our pages.

Marketing & Targeting Cookies

These cookies are set only with your explicit consent. They may be set through our website by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites. They work by uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services we use to enhance functionality and measure performance. These third parties include:

  • Google Analytics (Google LLC): Website traffic analytics and user behavior insights. Data is anonymized before processing.
  • Google Ads: Conversion tracking for advertising campaigns. Only activated with your consent.
  • Meta Pixel (Meta Platforms): Advertising and remarketing. Only activated with your consent.
  • Web3Forms: Contact form submission processing. Receives form data you voluntarily submit.
  • Google Fonts: Loads web fonts from Google's CDN. May log your IP address.
  • Font Awesome (Fonticons, Inc.): Loads icon assets. May log basic request data.

We do not control third-party cookies. Please review each third party's privacy and cookie policies for full information on how they handle data.

6. Cookie Duration

Cookies fall into two categories based on their lifespan:

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to keep you logged in during a single visit or to pass information between pages.
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (ranging from minutes to years) or until you delete them. They are used to remember your preferences across multiple visits.

The specific duration of each cookie is listed in the Cookie List table in Section 4. We do not set persistent cookies for longer than necessary to fulfil their stated purpose.

7. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies. Settings are typically found under "Privacy", "Security", or "Settings". Here are links to cookie management instructions for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

Our Cookie Consent Banner: When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can update your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

Opt-Out Tools: You can opt out of analytics cookies via the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on. For advertising cookies, visit the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out page.

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the site to operate securely.

8. Your Consent

When you first visit our website, we present a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies (including analytics and marketing)
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences by cookie category

We will not activate non-essential cookies (analytics, preference, or marketing) without your explicit consent. Your consent choices are stored in the cookie_consent cookie for 12 months, after which we will ask again.

You may withdraw or change your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies or contacting us at privacy@amiuriq.com. Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our use of cookies. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, by displaying a revised consent banner.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
